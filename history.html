Audit Methodology

The CashKeep audit engine utilizes a non-destructive retrieval method. Each record displayed above is a direct reflection of a JSON object stored in your browser's persistent memory node. Records are sorted by the Date.now() integer to ensure the most recent fiscal activity is presented at the top of the stack.

Deleting a record triggers a system-wide re-calculation of your net liquidity. This action is permanent and cannot be undone without a manual database re-entry.
